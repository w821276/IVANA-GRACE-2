Get To Know Ivana Grace

Ivana Grace is a singer/songwriter whose sound blends soul, hope, and empowerment into every lyric and melody. As a New Orleans based solo artist and songwriter, Ivana Grace is dedicated to blazing her own trail to success. Ivana Grace does't fit into a box. You can catch her singing sweet gospel melodies to soulful lyrics and then turning to country rhymes. She has even closed a concert up for the amazing Wyclef Jean at the House of Blues for his Vh1 Saves The Music Concert.
