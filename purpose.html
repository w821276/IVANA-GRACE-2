Passion Meets Purpose Heart Work

When passion meets purpose — that’s the perfect way to describe Ivana Grace. You can often find her behind the scenes, fighting the maternal health crisis in multiple roles: birth doula, certified breastfeeding specialist, Healthy Birth Ambassador, and childbirth educator. “Being a doula is heartwork for me,” says Ivana Grace. “As a doula, I wear many hats. I am always a prayer warrior to my Lord and Savior Jesus Christ, a chef, a psychologist, an advocate, a baby-name chooser, a housekeeper, and a shoulder to cry on. I am exactly what my mother needs in that moment.” Black women are dying at rates five to six times higher than others due to childbirth-related complications. Ivana Grace is committed to changing that narrative. She uses her platform to uplift, empower, and serve her community.

"It takes a village to raise a child — and a village to support a mother.."
— Ivana Grace
